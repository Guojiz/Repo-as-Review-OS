# Main Goal

Status: `active`

## Goal

Improve SAT Reading and Writing accuracy in 6 weeks by turning repeated mistakes into reusable models.

## Why it matters

SAT preparation often becomes a pile of completed practice questions.

A learning OS should not only record answers. It should help the student extract patterns, schedule review, and generate targeted practice from mistakes.

## Subgoals

- Track recurring mistake types.
- Build clean models without copying copyrighted questions.
- Review unstable patterns before they fade.
- Generate short targeted practice sets.
- Keep progress visible in one dashboard.

## Automations

- Repository organizer: check missing source records, stale mistake models, and overdue review items.
- Practice generator: create short review prompts based on weak patterns and next review dates.
