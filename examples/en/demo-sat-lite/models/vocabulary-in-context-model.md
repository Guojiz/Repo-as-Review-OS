## Model

For vocabulary in context questions, do not choose a word because it is generally related to the topic.

Choose the word that matches the exact local meaning required by the sentence.

## Recognition cue

When the answer choices all seem related, slow down and ask:

```text
What does the sentence require this word to do?
```

## Method

1. Cover the answer choices.
2. Predict the meaning from the sentence.
3. Find the clue that controls the meaning.
4. Eliminate choices that only match the topic.
5. Choose the word that matches the local function.

## Common trap

Topic match is not meaning match.

A word can sound related to the passage but still fail in the sentence.

## Review prompt

Create a new sentence with a blank. Then test whether the student can predict the required meaning before seeing choices.
