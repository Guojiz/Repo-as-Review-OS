# Main Goal

Status: `active`

## Goal

Understand and compare three papers about AI-assisted learning systems in two weeks.

## Why it matters

Research reading often fails when notes stay as isolated summaries.

This goal requires the AI to turn reading into reusable concepts, comparison points, review tasks, and open questions.

## Subgoals

- Create one source record for each paper.
- Extract the main claim of each paper.
- Build reusable concept models.
- Compare the papers across shared dimensions.
- Review key claims before they fade.

## Automations

- Repository organizer: check missing source records, stale notes, and unclear concept files.
- Review generator: create short review prompts from claims, concepts, and unanswered questions.
