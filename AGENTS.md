# AGENTS.md

[中文接手规则](AGENTS.zh-CN.md)

This file is for ChatGPT, Codex, and other AI agents. Follow it before editing this repository.

## Mission

Repo as Review OS is a reusable review-system template.

Your job is to help users turn scattered study material into a structured repository with sources, models, templates, dashboards, and handoff rules.

## Read first

1. `README.md`
2. `docs/architecture.md`
3. `docs/privacy-and-sourcing.md`
4. `agents/handoff-protocol.md`
5. Relevant files in `templates/`

## Do

- Keep original sources traceable.
- Mark incomplete files as `todo`.
- Keep English and Chinese in separate files when possible.
- Prefer small, reviewable changes.
- Explain changed files after editing.

## Do not

- Do not invent missing study context.
- Do not treat a summary as a source.
- Do not publish private examples.
- Do not remove user data unless explicitly asked.
- Do not mix English and Chinese line by line in long documents.

## Output format

After work, report:

1. Files changed;
2. What changed;
3. What remains.
